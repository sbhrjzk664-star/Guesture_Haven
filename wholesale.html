Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum bulk order is 100 units. Custom orders below this can be arranged for enterprise clients.

Do you offer customization?

Yes! We offer custom packaging, branding, and white-label options for orders 500+ units.

What's your delivery timeframe?

Standard: 5-7 business days. Express: 2-3 business days. Expedited options available for enterprise orders.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Absolutely! Discounts start at 25% for 100+ units and increase to 40% for 2000+ units.

Can I mix products in bulk orders?

Yes, you can create custom assortments. Minimum 100 units per product type applies.

Is there a dedicated account manager?

Yes, all wholesale accounts (500+ units annually) get a dedicated account manager for support and orders.
